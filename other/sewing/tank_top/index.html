Tank top

Title: Tank top
Description: A tank top for mum
Fabric: Cotton jersey
Date: August 2024

My mum was visiting and I made her a tank top. She wanted a tank top with longer sides, similar to one she already has.

The most challenging part of this project was the bottom hem. Since the sides are longer, the bottom edges were not perpendicular to the side seam. I had to improvise sewing those corners, but they turned out not too bad.
