Patch Shirt

Title: Patch Shirt
Description: A shirt
Fabric: Cotton jersey
Date: April 2024

This is the first item of clothing I made.

It was also the most challenging sewing project so far. I had never worked with stretchy fabrics before and I don’t have a walking foot, so it took some trial and error before I was able to get anything resembling a “straight” zig-zag stitch.

I also had a small accident while I was sewing a hem, which resulted in a small hole.
